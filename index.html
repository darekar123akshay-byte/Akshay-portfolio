About

I'm Akshay Darekar

I am a detail-oriented Clinical SAS Programmer with a unique foundation in pharmaceutical sciences, holding a Bachelor of Pharmacy degree. My career is driven by a passion for clinical research and a commitment to data integrity. I bridge the gap between raw clinical data and regulatory insights by combining my pharmacological knowledge with technical expertise in Base and Advanced SAS. Through a dedicated apprenticeship, I have honed my skills in the creation and validation of SDTM and ADaM datasets, ensuring compliance with CDISC standards. My project experience includes end-to-end clinical data analysis—from data cleaning and dataset generation to the production of critical reports and Define-XML documentation. I have applied these skills to high-impact areas, including the analysis of COVID-19 data.

My goal is to transform complex clinical trial data into meaningful, submission-ready insights that support sound regulatory decisions and ultimately advance patient health.
